Hi, my name is Jonatan—though many online know me as dotMavriQ. My journey into tech began in the mid-90s with Windows 95, but it was discovering Game Maker on a CD from a Swedish PC magazine that truly sparked my love for coding and digital creation. That passion grew into web development, starting with static XHTML sites and expanding into more dynamic builds with CSS and PHP.

In college, I built websites for local businesses, earned a Silver Medal at the Vara UF-mässa, and completed a formative traineeship at Nordic Goods, working hands-on with Linux servers, Magento, and SEO.

My career then expanded at Ember and Duva, where I moved from WordPress to full-stack development—mastering Docker, Kubernetes, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind. I also served as President of Makerspace Lidköping, contributing nationally with web and tech solutions. Later, at IPTOR and Block Labs, I sharpened my skills in marketing-focused development and rapid application development.

Today, I’m the Senior Growth Developer at Nordhealth, where I extend the Marketing Team by maintaining and improving digital properties, designing robust deployments, and implementing new features for an emerging AI-driven healthcare leader.

What drives me is a relentless curiosity and a commitment to delivering user-centered solutions that work. My diverse experience, combined with a genuine passion for technology, makes me a reliable problem-solver—someone who brings both technical expertise and a thoughtful, human approach to every project.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing bass guitar, cooking, reading, trekking, and spending quality time with my partner.
